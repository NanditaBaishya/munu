This is a traditional snake game

Aim: To eat as many fruits as possible in the grid by the snake without biting it's tail

The tail increases by a unit every time the snake consumes the fruit
 
Movements:
w-UP
d-RIGHT
a-LEFT
s-DOWN

x-To quit the game at any time

Your score will be updated by addition of  10 points everytime the snake consumes 
the fruit

The two ways for game over
1.Snake biting it's own tail
2.You press the key ' x ' to quit the game
